# Basic Nmap Scan

## 🔍 Command Used

nmap 192.168.1.41

## 📘 Purpose

To discover open ports on the local machine.

## 📊 Observation

The scan displayed a list of open ports and their states (open/closed/filtered).

## 🧠 Learning

Basic scans help identify exposed services that could be potential entry points for attackers.
